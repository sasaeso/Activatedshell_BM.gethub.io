Value Propositions

About activated shell project

The primary value proposition is the significant reduction of CO2 concentration in indoor spaces, such as school kitchens, offices, factories, and public spaces. This addresses a pressing environmental issue by providing a sustainable solution for air purification. Also, the project depends on waste materials (eggshells and coal) to create useful products (calcium hydroxide and activated carbon), promoting a circular economy and reducing environmental impact.

Customer Segments

STEM School

Start with STEM schools, especially in kitchen areas where CO2 is generated during cooking. Schools are always looking to provide a cleaner, healthier environment for students and staff












factories

For factories, especially those in manufacturing, food processing, or chemical production, CO2 is frequently emitted as a byproduct of various processes. In addition to CO2, other hazardous air pollutants (HAPs) such as volatile organic compounds (VOCs), nitrogen oxides (NOx), and particulate matter (PM) can be generated. Prolonged exposure to these pollutants can cause respiratory issues, fatigue, and decreased cognitive performance among workers. Factories, in particular, need reliable solutions that can remove or reduce the concentration of CO2 and other harmful substances to maintain a safe and healthy environment.

Workplaces

Workplaces, especially in high-density offices or areas with large numbers of people, also have a demand for air quality control systems. In densely populated spaces, CO2 levels can rise quickly, leading to discomfort, reduced concentration, and overall reduced well-being among employees. Businesses, particularly those with open-plan offices, often seek to optimize air quality to improve employee engagement, reduce sick days, and boost productivity.

Cost Structure

The project incurs several key costs associated with its development and operation. The initial building of the project, including infrastructure and setup, requires an investment of 32,000 L.E. Raw materials, including chemicals such as eggshells and coal for the filtration systems, cost approximately 5,000 L.E. Ongoing maintenance of the systems and customer support services is estimated at 5,000 L.E per year. Marketing and sales expenses, covering advertising, promotions, and the sales team's salary, will be 15,000 L.E. Additionally, shipping costs for delivering the products to customers are a significant part of the distribution and logistics budget. The total cost will be approximately between 59,000 and 100,000 L.E, as it will vary every year.
